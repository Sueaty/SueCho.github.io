'Miss You' is an app created as part of a class project in Software Engineering course. This app aims to reduce the number of lost pets. If a person finds a seemingly lost pet, the person can post picture, and description of it. I was in charge of our group's dev team, drawing the major outline and functions of the app. I particularly took part in, authentication and data storing and retrieval using firebase.

Dodam Dodam (Android Application)

'Dodam Dodam' is an app that my team had worked for 4 months and won the second place in 6th K-Hackathon. This app aims to solve some common questions that women in their pregancy have with a chatbot. I was the leader of the group taking part in gathering(crawling) question data from Korean communities for pregnant women and finding out frequnently asked questions. I also took part in dealing with the firebase.
